Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Avira AI Reels Creator ("the App") is developed and maintained by Prashant. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to generate AI-powered video reels and does not collect or sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

The app collects minimal information required to function:

  • Topic text you enter to generate reels (processed on our server, not stored permanently)
  • Device storage access — only to save downloaded videos to your device
  • Internet access — required to connect to our backend server and generate reels

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not store your usage history on any external server
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels

Third Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com
  • Pexels API — used to fetch stock video clips for reel generation
  • Google Gemini API — used to generate reel scripts using AI

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — to connect to backend server for reel generation
  • READ/WRITE EXTERNAL STORAGE — to save generated videos to your Downloads folder

Data Security

All communication between the app and our server is done over HTTP. Generated video files are temporarily stored on our server and served to your device. We do not permanently store any user-generated content.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
